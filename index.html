I am currently pursuing a PhD in Industrial Engineering at Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya · Barcelona Tech (UPC). I am a prospective PhD student, applying for fall 2023 admission. My research interests lie primarily in Operation Research, Reinforcement Learning, Supply Chain, Manufacturing, and Transportation.
